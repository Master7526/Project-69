Lowe Elementary

I had Visited in August 14, 2015

Lowe Elementary School is a highly rated, public school located in Louisville, KY. It has 583 students in grades K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 18 to 1. According to state test scores, 71% of students are at least proficient in math and 78% in reading.
